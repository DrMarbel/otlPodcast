What is "On The Lectern"?

On The Lectern is a variety show podcast. The topics are random but will be the sole focus in each episode, and, sometimes, I'll have a guest on the show to talk with me about certain topics. The show is a sort of passion project and a hobby-in-the-making.

Who pulls the strings?

As far as who runs the show, that would be none other than our very own: Dr. Marbel! Obviously, that is an internet handle, but, while online, he often responds to Doc! He's also the leader of the discord server The Embassy!
